About Mashirikiano SACCO

Mashirikiano SACCO is a member-owned financial cooperative committed to uplifting youth, men, and women through accessible savings, credit, and investment opportunities. We provide practical financial solutions, member education, and responsible support that helps families and businesses grow with confidence.

  • OUR VISION

    To be the most trusted SACCO in Kenya and the East African region, known for excellence in service delivery, innovation, and member empowerment..

  • OUR PURPOSE

    We empower our members to live with dignity and accomplishment throughout their lives. Empowerment is about members experiencing more joy of saving, having peace of mind because they are confident to face financial uncertainties; and satisfaction and fulfilment because they are part of a SACCO that cares and provides them with opportunities for advancement and personal growth.

  • OUR CORE VALUES

    Accountability & Empowerment: We hold people accountable for performance and We give our people the legitimate right to make judgement, form conclusions, reach decisions, and then act. Service & Empathy: We are obsessed with serving our members and We step into the shoes of our members to understand their feelings and perspectives, and use that understanding to guide our actions.
