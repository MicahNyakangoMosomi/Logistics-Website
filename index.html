About Mashirikiano SACCO

Mashirikiano SACCO is a member-owned financial cooperative committed to uplifting youth, men, and women through accessible savings, credit, and investment opportunities. We provide practical financial solutions, member education, and responsible support that helps families and businesses grow with confidence.

  • OUR VISION

    To be the most trusted SACCO in Kenya and the East African region, known for excellence in service delivery, innovation, and member empowerment..

  • OUR PURPOSE

    We empower our members to live with dignity and accomplishment throughout their lives. Empowerment is about members experiencing more joy of saving, having peace of mind because they are confident to face financial uncertainties; and satisfaction and fulfilment because they are part of a SACCO that cares and provides them with opportunities for advancement and personal growth.

  • OUR CORE VALUES

    Accountability & Empowerment: We hold people accountable for performance and We give our people the legitimate right to make judgement, form conclusions, reach decisions, and then act. Service & Empathy: We are obsessed with serving our members and We step into the shoes of our members to understand their feelings and perspectives, and use that understanding to guide our actions.

Membership Deposits

Member deposits are a form of savings/Investment: Used to borrow loan x3 or x4 Used to gurantee loans for other members Incase of death, the Sink fund (insurance) scheme doubles the deposits of the deceased member and pays it to the next of kin

Karibu Loan

Looking to establish yourself quickly and begin your journey toward financial freedom after joining our SACCO? The Karibu Loan is your ideal solution. True to its name ("Karibu" meaning "welcome"), this product is specifically designed to help new members access development capital - even with limited savings - without waiting through the traditional 6-month period. With the Karibu Loan, you can immediately start turning your aspirations into reality.

Learn More

Development Loan

Looking to fund an important investment or purchase property? Our Development Loan offers a cost-effective, multipurpose financing solution designed to help members improve or fully finance their investments at competitive, affordable rates.

Learn More

Emergency Loan

Financial emergencies don't have to be stressful. Our Emergency Loan provides members with quick and convenient access to funds when unexpected needs arise. We prioritize rapid disbursement, understanding that urgent situations demand immediate solutions. Experience the peace of mind that comes with knowing financial support is just moments away.

Learn More

Salary In Advance

When urgent financial needs arise, our Salary-In-Advance provides a quick solution for members whose salaries are processed through FOSA. This short-term facility gives you access to funds when you need them most, with convenient repayment from your next salary
