How to Pay via M-PESA

Follow these simple steps from your Safaricom line to deposit money securely into your Mashirikiano SACCO account.

Go to M-PESA Menu

Open your Safaricom Sim Tool Kit or M-PESA app and select Lipa na M-PESA.

Select Paybill

Choose the Paybill option from the menu.

Enter Business Number

Enter Mashirikiano SACCO's official business number:

4048759
Enter Account Number

Use your National ID Number as the account number.

Your ID No.
(e.g. 12345678)
Complete Transaction

Enter the Amount you wish to deposit, input your M-PESA PIN, and click OK. You will receive a confirmation SMS shortly.
