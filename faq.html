Frequently Asked Questions

Mashirikiano SACCO has served members for years through a stable cooperative model focused on savings discipline, responsible lending, and member empowerment.

Membership is open to eligible individuals who complete an application form and submit the required identification documents. Our team guides every applicant through onboarding and account activation.

We review your income flow, savings history, and goals to recommend suitable options, including deposit growth, development financing, emergency support, and salary advance facilities.

We offer products such as Karibu Loan, Development Loan, Emergency Loan, and Salary-In-Advance for eligible members. Each product has clear terms to match different financial needs.

Loan limits depend on your savings history, product type, and SACCO policy. In most cases, members qualify for a multiple of their deposits after meeting product requirements.

Our philosophy is member-first service, integrity in every transaction, and shared prosperity through cooperative action.

We use structured internal controls, approved operating policies, and professional oversight to safeguard member contributions, personal information, and all SACCO records.

We operate under recognized cooperative standards and governance practices, and we continuously improve compliance to protect member interests and trust.

You can reach us through phone, email, or by visiting our office in Thika, Kenya. Our support team is available to assist with accounts, loans, and general member inquiries.
