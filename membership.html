Be a Member

REQUIREMENTS

An aspiring member is required to:

  • Provide the dully filled membership application form accompanied by a copy of their national ID (Passport for non-Kenyans), employment letter or pay slip and a passport size photograph.
  • For kin members, a birth certificate (for children membership) or marriage certificate/marriage affidavit (for spouse membership) is required.

BENEFITS OF BEING A MEMBER

Join Mashirikiano SACCO for finacial exellence:

  • Opportunity to save
  • High returns on investment
  • Access to affordable credit products
  • Education on proper management of finances and investment practices

MEMBERSHIP IS OPEN TO

Who are able to join the sacco?:

  • Any Kenyan Citizen
  • Immediate family members of the primary members – Spouses and Children
